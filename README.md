# Privacy Policy

**Gujarati Dictation by Dave Sir (com.shivaminstitute)**

**Effective Date:** March 25, 2026

Gujarati Dictation by Dave Sir is a mobile application developed to help students and aspirants improve their shorthand (steno) writing speed through structured dictation practice. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## **1. Information We Collect**

We collect certain types of information to provide and improve our services:

### **Personal Data**
While using our service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information. This includes:
* **Email Address:** Used for account creation, authentication, and communication.
* **Full Name (First and Last name):** To personalize your experience.
* **Phone Number:** For account verification and support.
* **Location Data (City & State):** To understand our user base and tailor regional content if applicable.
* **Authentication Data:** We use Supabase for secure authentication.

### **Usage Data**
We may collect information on how the app is accessed and used, including:
* **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
* **FCM Token:** Used to send push notifications for updates, new dictations, and announcements.
* **App Usage:** Features accessed, time spent, and audio streaming/download activity.

---

## **2. Permissions Required**

To provide a seamless experience, the app requires the following permissions:
* **Internet Access:** To stream dictation audio, sync user data, and receive notifications.
* **Wake Lock & Foreground Service:** To ensure uninterrupted audio playback when the screen is off or the app is in the background.
* **Post Notifications:** To send important alerts and updates (Android 13+).

---

## **3. Third-Party Services**

We use trusted third-party services to handle specific functionalities:
* **Supabase:** For backend database management, user authentication, and data storage.
* **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):** For delivering push notifications.
* **Just Audio:** For high-quality audio playback and background service management.

These third parties have their own privacy policies and are only allowed to use your data to provide these services to us.

---

## **4. Use of Data**

Gujarati Dictation by Dave Sir uses the collected data for:
* Providing and maintaining app functionality.
* Managing user accounts and authentication.
* Delivering dictation audio streams and offline downloads.
* Improving app performance and user experience.
* Sending push notifications for updates and announcements.
* Providing technical support and resolving issues.

---

## **5. Data Security**

We prioritize the security of your data. We use industry-standard encryption and secure backend services (Supabase) to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## **6. Your Rights & Data Retention**

* **Data Access & Deletion:** You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
* **Opt-out:** You can opt-out of push notifications through your device settings.
* **Offline Data:** Downloaded audio files remain on your device and can be manually deleted at any time.

---

## **7. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app. Your continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
